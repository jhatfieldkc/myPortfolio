About Me

Where I'm From

My name is Jeremy and I am a web development student in Kansas City. I grew up helping with the family bakery which taught me a lot about small local business. After leaving my job in 2019 I decided I wanted to help other local businesses which has lead me to working with web development. I believe that small business is the key to local economic growth and sustainability.

Where I'm Going

My love of small business has lead me to wanting to help them grow through quality web design and support. After spending way too much time trying to work with free site builders, I realized that a great website really needs to be designed specifically for the business. I also believe technology should work with you and not against you which means proper integration with their current systems.
